Brief History of Gunungkidul

Gunungkidul Regency is one of 5 regencies/cities in the Special Region of Yogyakarta (DIY). The distance from Yogyakarta City to Wonosari City is approximately 40 km to the southeast. The total area of Gunungkidul is 1,485.36 km² or approximately 46.63% of the total area of DIY. Gunungkidul Regency is divided into 18 sub-districts covering 144 villages and 1,431 hamlets with the district capital in Wonosari.

The area of Gunungkidul Regency is located between 7° 46'- 8° 09' South Latitude and 110° 21' - 110° 50' East Longitude, which is bordered by Klaten Regency, Sukoharjo Regency, Central Java in the north. Wonogiri Regency, Central Java in the east. Indonesian Ocean in the south and Bantul Regency, Sleman Regency, DI Yogyakarta in the west. The total area of Gunungkidul Regency is 1,485.36 km² which includes 18 sub-districts and 144 villages/kelurahan. Semanu District is the largest sub-district with an area of about 108.39 km² or about 7.30 percent of the area of Gunungkidul Regency.
